We are a team of high school students who know what it is like to struggle to fall asleep. Innovation and modern technology have allowed us to develop special insertable pods capable of different features. Our product will help you get the sleep you want and deserve.

Why choose us?

A good night's sleep is vital for every human being to survive. Given that an average a person sleeps for 8 hours in a day, that means that an average person will sleep for 229,961 hours in their lifetime or basically one third of their life. This is a immense amount of a persons lifetime and it should be spent correctly by getting the sleep everyone deserves!
